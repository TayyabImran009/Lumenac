  WHO WE ARE?

Lumenac has been present for years among the leading companies in the lighting market. For 37 years, it has produced and manufactured in Argentina a wide range of products for industrial, commercial and residential lighting. The products that Lumenac offers are manufactured in its two production plants located in the town of Villa Martelli, Buenos Aires.
